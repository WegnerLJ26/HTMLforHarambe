Our Signature Drinks

Woparita

The Woparita has been a staple of Mark's Margs every since the company was founded. It is a huge hit on the University of Wisconsin - Whitewater campus. There are many different ways to make this drink and students have put there own twist on it. The Woparita is so famous though because of Mr. Margarita's secret ingredient.

Tropical Marg

The Tropical Marg is the perfect drink to pass around during a sunny day cooking out at the local Whitewater beach.

The Caliente Marg

The Caliente Marg is for all those spicey food lovers out there. You'll spend the rest of your day with an extra hop in your step.

The Mamacita

The Mamacita is right behind the Woparita in popularity. Mr. Margarita, being the gentleman and scholar he is, wanted to create a drink especially for the ladies. The result was the Mamacita. Perfect for any ladies night out!

Morning Sunrise

The Morning Sunrise is the perfect drink for a hungover Saturday morning. It will energize you and wake you up for a long day of productivity!
